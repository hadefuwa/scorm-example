Prototype Mode

Upload one tagged Word source and see what the pipeline would do.

This demo extracts readable text from a local .docx, detects custom tags, lists the blocks in source order, proposes outputs, and highlights missing SCORM decisions without claiming compliance.

1. Upload source Pick a tagged Word file from your machine. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
2. Detect structure Preview the document, find tags, filenames, warnings, and likely classifications.
3. Show outcomes See proposed HTML or DOCX-style outputs plus what SCORM still needs.

What This Checks

Tags <HTML>, <worksheet>, <document>, and <filename>.
Blocks Source order, tag type, filename, excerpt, and likely classification.
Outputs Likely destination folders such as outputs/html/ and outputs/docx/.
SCORM Version, launch file, manifest, completion, tracking, and sequencing gaps.
