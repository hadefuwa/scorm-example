Prototype Vs Production

Right now the project demonstrates evidence-led control, not full automation.

Prototype mode now

Initial evidence available

  • Shows real reports, real extracted files, and real built outputs.
  • Stops when filenames, extensions, or SCORM decisions are unresolved.
  • Keeps teacher content out of learner output folders.
Production mode later

Repeatable and fully evidenced

  • Completes all 28 learner-facing outputs, not just a prototype slice.
  • Assembles browser delivery from the full built set.
  • Packages SCORM only after explicit decisions are confirmed and validated.
